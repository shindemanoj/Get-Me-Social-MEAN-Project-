About the product

We wish to connect people who share a common interest. We have the following three categories as of now, hence any user who has these interests can see the local events created by the other users within 2 mile radius.

Sports

Users interested in sports can create an event or a match, so that other users from the same field of interest are able to see the event and participate. We have a live chat feature in which all the participants can discuss about the meeting time,point etc. You can also view the participants who are coming to the event. For the user convenience we have the route from your location to the event place on the google maps. Also, the event owner can edit and delete the event. The events get deleted automatically if the date and time of the event has passed.

Movies

Users interested in movies can post the movie name, its image and write posts of how he feels about the movie. Also, he may give the rating to a movie. He may edit the movie ratings and description later on, if he his opinion has changed about it. We also have the live chat feature where discussion about the movie can take place.






Restaurants

Users interested in food and who like to review about a particular item, or who would like others to know what to buy and is worth the money will find this very useful. The user can even post a real-time image of the food and rate it. As always, we do provide a live chat feature for the users response on the review.
